About Us

We have been creating beautiful smiles for the last 6 months in our state of the art Belfast practice in Northern Ireland. Our dental philosophy is based upon two key aspects. The first is to provide the best standards of care available in the modern market, and the second is to pioneer the most effective, innovative treatments around. At Jenny's Dental Practice, you will find the perfect treatment, tailored to you, in order to deliver a simply beautiful, natural looking smile. We offer a whole range of cosmetic dental and orthodontic treatments, from veneers, tooth whitening and crowns, to Invisalign and Six Month Smiles. Our dedication to all patients and skill is evidenced in our work.
At Jenny's Dental Practice we are dedicated to offering the highest quality dental care possible. We deliver this by utilising the latest technology, procedures and techniques combined with a dedicated team of dental professionals who undertake continuing education programmes to maintain and grow our clinical skills. We aim to offer a truly unique service designed to exceed our patient's expectations and build not only beautiful smiles but greater self esteem!
